The Current State, Disconnected Players with No Center
Fragmented Communication
Growers, brands, retailers, and budtenders operate in complete silos. No unified voice, no shared intelligence layer, and no mechanism for aligned growth across the supply chain.
Platform Censorship Risk
Instagram, TikTok, and Meta aggressively suppress cannabis content. Artists and brands are one policy change away from losing everything they have built on these platforms.
No Product Education Pipeline
With 1,000 or more new SKUs per quarter in Ontario alone, budtenders cannot stay educated and brands have no direct engagement mechanism. Knowledge retention is broken at the point of sale.
Artists Cannot Find Growers
Musicians and creative talent want cannabis brand partnerships but cannot navigate the industry. The cultural crossover between cannabis and music is massive and completely unmonetized.
No Industry Data Layer
Brands have no insight into budtender demographics, regional behavior, or product sentiment at scale. Marketing decisions are made without the data intelligence that could transform outcomes.
US Expansion Has No Infrastructure
As state by state legalization accelerates, operators are entering new markets without any platform designed to help the cannabis community scale nationally and build lasting community.

Proof of Concept, Boardy AI
Market Validation
Boardy AI proved the model. An AI that makes warm introductions at conferences and networking events went viral in the technology world with zero ad spend and pure word of mouth, because people genuinely valued AI mediated connections. Mairy Jane is that exact concept deployed into the cannabis and music industry, where the need is far greater and the competition is nonexistent.

Boardy AI
Boardy is an AI that makes introductions between people across its network. One conversation, the AI matches you with relevant contacts, and warm introductions happen automatically with full consent from both parties. It became the defining conversation at technology conferences in 2024 and 2025 without spending a dollar on marketing.

The Capital Advisory and Lending Partnership
SET Ventures, WAVMVMT, Direct Lender Access
SET Ventures and SET Enterprises, Capital Advisory and Lending
The other $500K is not just working capital. It is the start of an ongoing growth relationship with direct access to cannabis lenders, MCA providers, and capital across the United States and Canada.
Through SET Ventures and SET Enterprises, led by Chris Marchese with capital advisory from WAVMVMT, the LP partner gains access to an ongoing lending, structuring, and growth relationship operating across multiple verticals in both the United States and Canada. SET Ventures and WAVMVMT maintain direct relationships with cannabis lenders and MCA providers, meaning the LP does not need to search the market independently. The $500K working capital deployment is the entry point to a full service capital partnership built to grow as the LP grows, from initial MCA access through to commercial bridge lending, multi location expansion, and US market positioning.
Direct Cannabis Lender Access
SET Ventures and WAVMVMT hold direct relationships with cannabis lenders and MCA providers across Canada and the United States. The LP gains immediate access to this network without cold outreach or independent market searches.
Bridge Lending, $2M to $25M+
SET Ventures specializes in commercial bridge lending at scale. As the LP expands operations, locations, or markets, the next level of capital is available through an established relationship rather than a new process.
Multi Vertical Growth Support
The SET ecosystem covers capital advisory, fractional CMO placement through SET Marketing, deal origination, and investor relations across cannabis, real estate, and adjacent verticals in both the United States and Canada.
US Market Expansion Capital
As Rollagram enters US state markets in Phase 2 and the LP considers parallel expansion, SET Ventures provides deal origination, capital introductions, and market entry structuring with existing lender relationships already in place across key states.
